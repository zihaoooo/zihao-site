Zihao Zhang is an associate professor at the City College of New York Spitzer School of Architecture. His research focuses on cybernetics and the constructed environment — the entanglement of nature and technology, the human and nonhuman, ecosystems and intelligent machines. His book Cybernetics and the Constructed Environment (Routledge, 2024) interrogates the ramifications of cybernetics on contemporary culture and the built environment. He is also a co-founder of OSE (Office of Speculative Ecologies), a design practice working across climate adaptation, urban ecology, and social justice.

He holds a PhD in the Constructed Environment and an MLA from the University of Virginia, and a BEng in Landscape Architecture from Beijing Forestry University. He co-hosts Chubby Cat Happy Chat, a Mandarin podcast on design, philosophy, art, and technology.

张子豪现任纽约城市学院 Spitzer 建筑学院副教授，研究方向聚焦控制论与建成环境，探讨自然与技术、人类与非人类之间的共存关系。专著 Cybernetics and the Constructed Environment（Routledge, 2024）追问控制论如何重塑当代文化与建成环境。他同时是 OSE 的联合创始人，设计实践围绕气候适应、城市生态与社会公正展开。
